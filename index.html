Anshoo Tandon



I am a Research Fellow at the National University of Singapore (NUS). I am currently working with Prof. Vincent Tan (since Oct. 2018), and I have worked previously with Prof. Teng Joon Lim (Oct. 2017 - Sep. 2018), and with Prof. Mehul Motani (Jan. 2016 - Sep. 2017). I received the Ph.D. degree from NUS, where I was supervised by Prof. Mehul Motani (2012 - 2016). I received the M.E. degree from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, India, and the B.E. degree from Kumaun University, Nainital, India.

I have a rich industry experience of over 9 years, having worked in lead/senior roles with leading (telecom) semiconductor companies such as Broadcom, ARM, Freescale Semiconductor (merged with NXP), and Analog Devices.

My research interests span probabilistic graphical models, information theory, coding theory, signal processing, and wireless communications.
